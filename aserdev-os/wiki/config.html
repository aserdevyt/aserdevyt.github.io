Configuration

Document configuration files, dotfiles, and recommended settings here.

Hyprland

your hyprland stuff will be in ~/.config/hypr/

here is your main hyprland config

nvim ~/.config/hypr/hyprland.conf

here are your keybinds hyprland configs

nvim ~/.config/hypr/binds.conf

Rofi

Rofi config files are located in ~/.config/rofi/

here is your main rofi config

nvim ~/.config/rofi/config.rasi

Waybar

Waybar config files are located in ~/.config/waybar/

here is your main waybar config

nvim ~/.config/waybar/config

here is your style waybar config

nvim ~/.config/waybar/style.css

here is your color waybar config

nvim ~/.config/waybar/colors.css

pacman

here is your pacman config

sudo nvim /etc/pacman.conf
